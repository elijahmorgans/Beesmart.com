    What does a motivational speaker do?


    Motivational speakers give inspirational and informative speeches to groups of people. They are hired by businesses, schools,
    resorts, and communities to speak on topics such as achieving personal or financial success,
    living a healthy lifestyle, or organizing one's personal life or business.
